CARDIFF’S WINTER WONDERLAND

Cardiff’s much-loved Winter Wonderland returns to the Castle grounds and City Hall lawns with it’s famous undercover ice-rink and family funfair among a whole host of attractions. Alongside the alpine ski-lodge bar, Sur la Piste, festive food and drink stalls, not to mention the seriously cool Ice Bar, Winter Wonderland really is at the heart of the Cardiff’s seasonal celebrations.

CARDIFF CHRISTMAS MARKET

Visit the Christmas Market in Cardiff where, nestled around the city’s main shopping areas, you will find original work from talented makers in each and every stall. Curated by Craft*Folk, be sure to look out for bespoke jewellery, beautiful textiles, wooden turned gifts, and original artwork across all mediums. Combined with a variety of seasonal food and drink, you can be sure to enjoy a vibrant festive atmosphere.

CHRISTMAS EVENTS IN CARDIFF

Nothing helps the festive feeling like getting out and about for some fun with the family. As well as the usual Christmas shows and panto, Bute Park has an exciting outdoor light trail to look forward to. Along with our famous Winter Wonderland it really is most wonderful time of the year.

Your guide

Years ago, I left the vibrant warmth of Ghana, my hometown, and arrived in Cardiff a city I had never set foot in but would soon call home. From the moment I walked its cobbled streets, surrounded by the rhythm of a bustling yet welcoming community, I felt an unexpected connection. Cardiff, with its blend of modernity and history, its serene parks, and its spirited people, embraced me in ways I couldn’t have imagined. It wasn’t just a place to live; it became a part of me; a city that nurtured my growth, celebrated my differences, and made me fall in love with its unique charm.
